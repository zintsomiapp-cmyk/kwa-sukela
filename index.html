About Dr. Sasa

A Voice That Carries History

With a Master of Arts in Storytelling from Tennessee State University and a PhD in Folklore from the University of the Witwatersrand, Dr. Nomsa Mdlalose has spent over two decades at the intersection of tradition and teaching.

Known across Southern Africa as "Dr. Sasa," she is the founder of Zintsomi, the creator of Africa's first accredited storytelling course, and an Imbokodo Award-winning voice for African women's narratives.

What We Offer

Our Services

From captivating live performances to professional mentorship programmes, Kwa Sukela serves storytellers, educators, and organisations across Africa and beyond.

Live Performances
Electrifying storytelling performances for festivals, conferences, and cultural events. Dr. Sasa brings African oral tradition to life for audiences of all ages.
Workshops & Training
Immersive storytelling workshops for individuals, schools, and organisations. Tailored curricula that build practical skills grounded in African tradition.
Mentorship
One-on-one guidance for emerging storytellers. Dr. Sasa works directly with mentees on voice, repertoire, performance technique, and career development.
Books & Publications
A growing collection of African storytelling books, anthologies, and educational materials authored and curated by Dr. Sasa.
Digital Storytelling
Audio recordings, video productions, and digital storytelling resources that bring African narratives to global digital audiences.
School Programmes
Curriculum-aligned storytelling programmes for primary and secondary schools, integrating African oral tradition into the modern classroom.
