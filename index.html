Our Founder

Dr. Nomsa Mdlalose
‚Äî Dr. Sasa

üèÜ Imbokodo Award 2015 ‚Äî African Languages & Storytelling
"It is through the arts that we preserve our languages, culture and history in a non-conflicting manner."

Dr. Nomsa Mdlalose is a folklorist, researcher, writer, storyteller, and social entrepreneur. She holds a Storytelling Master's from Tennessee State University (USA) and a Doctorate in Folklore from Wits University, specialising in African Oral Literature and Linguistics.

With over 20 years of experience, Dr. Sasa has performed and facilitated storytelling across South Africa and internationally. She founded Kwa Sukela Storytelling Academy and Zintsomi ‚Äî South Africa's leading storytelling hub dedicated to preserving African stories and their tellers.

She has served on the literature panel of the National Arts Council of South Africa, Freedom Park Council, and is a board member of the Southern African Folklore Society.
